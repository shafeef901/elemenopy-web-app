So, who are we?

Simply put, we are a space for discovery and learning; created by experienced educators who believe children need a nurturing yet engaging environment to grow in.



And with growth, comes change. Children are constantly transitioning as they develop, and at Elemenopy, they find themselves in a happy, creative and secure space which prepares them to make these transitions smoothly.

What does a mother do when she sees that there is no quality education available for her children?

Well, Mariyam Abdul Azeez was not the one to give into circumstances. As a young mother, when she saw he lack of good educational facilities in Jeddah, where she had settled with her family, she decided to take matters into her own hands and became a teacher at her son’s school. This was the start of her long and inspirational journey in the field of education.

TEAM



ROHINI ANAND

She believes, teaching is not only her passion but her calling. Doing her best so that her students can be the best version of themselves, is the purpose she is committed to. And to achieve this, she focuses on innovative, hands-on activities that make learning fun and exciting for her students.

SUJATA

As a mother of two, Sujata is more than aware of the importance of early childhood education. Hence, with a teaching approach that nurtures the imagination and creativity of children, she is committed to helping them become confident yet sensitive individuals during these formative years.

MAJEEHA

A qualified Montessori and CIDTT trained teacher, Majeeha has worked for more than seven years in the field of early childhood education. Believing herself to be more of a facilitator than a teacher, she helps children explore, discover and understand by creating a learning environment that unleashes their minds.

ARTHI

An administrator par excellence, Arthi has also been a yoga instructor for children for four years. After studying yoga and Bharatanatyam at Kalakshethra and graduating in the same from Tamilnadu Music college, she completed the Arkaya Trainers Program with Yogacharini Maitreyi; post which she started teaching both the arts and continues to do so till today.
